Follow the steps to pay Fees

- Go to M-PESA on your phone.
- Select Pay Bill option.
- Enter Business no. 886858.
- Enter student Name/Reg Number as the account number.
- Enter the Amount.
- Enter your M-PESA PIN and Send.
- You will receive a confirmation via SMS.
- Payment reflects within 15 minutes on our side.
