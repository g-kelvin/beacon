ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

The school was started from scratch on September 2013. Only one pupil reported after one week. That term we registered at least 11pupils. In January 2014 We registered more pupils. Two years down the line we had a population of 51 kids.Glory to God. Currently we have a population of approximately 250kids We did our first KCPE in 2019.Out of 17 more than 10pupils joined Extra county schools.
Year 2021 we have our third KCPE. Some of our kids in 2020 KCPE were hilighted by the standard news paper for impressive performance. We are rated by Google,Top Five best academies in Embu. We are the youngest fastest upcoming school in Embu town which has done at least Two KCPEs in a very short duration. We are the first school private in Embu to have bought a brand new 33seater Isuzu bus in just less than five years from inception. By this year we have already integrated digital literacy in our learning including the CBC classes.
